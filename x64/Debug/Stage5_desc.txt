You found a long narrow tunnel. It is the only way left you can find that goes further deeper 
into the dungeon. As you go deep inside, you notice that there are no signs of living things 
anymore. You can hear some evil whisper, you hope it is just a sign of tiredness. Eventually, 
you find something at the end of the tunnel. It looks like a sanctuary, except there are skulls 
surrounding it. Knowing this would be the end of the journey, you step on towards the sanctuary. 
As you get close to it, you notice that a person is waiting for you there...
